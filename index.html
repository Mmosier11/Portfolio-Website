Current Game Projects

Project Equinox

This is my current game project that I am working on. Project Equinox is a 3rd person, action RPG, centered around player decision. This has been a passion project of mine for the past couple of years. Amongst hashing out the story and dialogue I have been brainstorming what game engine to use, what graphics style I should implement, and what the gameplay should be.

Eventually, I settled for using the Unity Engine as it is the engine that I have the most experience with. Among my search for the perfect graphics, I stumbled upon Synty Studios Polygon assets. Now all thats left is for me to implement and flesh out gameplay features and finish my story!
